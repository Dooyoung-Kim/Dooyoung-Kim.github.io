Director

Dr. Dooyoung Kim

Lecturer, Dept. of CS & IT, La Trobe University

Founding director of IXRI Lab and Lecturer in Computer Science & Information Technology at La Trobe University. PhD in Culture Technology (AR/VR) from KAIST. Research interests include XR for Physical AI, XRMemory, spatial AI, HCI, and intelligent interfaces. 29+ publications, 9 patents, Best Paper Awards at IEEE ISMAR (Top 1%), and Co-PI on $9M+ in national grants. Lead Guest Editor for Springer VR (XRMemory), Global Shaper at the World Economic Forum.
