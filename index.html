Summary

I am a Senior Researcher (Ph.D.) at KAIST and a Visiting Researcher at NYU, dedicated to bridging physical and virtual worlds through Spatial Computing and XR Telepresence. My research vision is to connect people beyond space and time by developing next-generation immersive platforms.

Currently, I serve as a Co-Principal Investigator (Co-PI) on a $3M National R&D Project (Meta-Object) and as a System Integration Manager for a $6M OpenXR Platform Project, directing over 40 researchers across multi-disciplinary teams. My research excellence has been recognized with three Best Paper Awards at IEEE ISMAR (Top 1% in 2025 x2, 1st Prize in 2024), the premier conference in Augmented Reality.

Beyond research, I actively drive global initiatives as a Project Leader for the KAIST-NYU Meta-Museum collaboration and as a pioneer of the "XRMemory" concept (Lead Guest Editor for Springer VR).
